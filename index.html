About us

Cable Glide is a regional high-speed transport system. Wing-supported cabins ride two slim rails carried on cables between slender pylons, crossing terrain, water and roads with a minimal footprint.

Our goal is to bring high-speed connections to routes where conventional high-speed rail is too costly or impossible to build — not competing with mainline trains but complementing them, with light, inexpensive, elevated infrastructure and frequent service.

The vehicle's wings carry much of the cabin weight in flight, removing vertical load from the track and allowing high speed on slender infrastructure. An onboard turbulence-cancelling system keeps the ride smooth and holds vehicle–cable forces within safe limits.
